1995 Mustang Cobra R
SN95

  • Engine: 351 Windsor V8 5.8L.
  • Horsepower: 300 hp @ 4,800 rpm.
  • Torque: 365 lb/ft @ 3,750 rpm.
  • Weight: 3,326 lbs.
  • Transmission: Tremec TR-3550 5-speed.
  • Front Brakes: 13 in - vented cast-iron disc - twin-piston caliper.
  • Rear Brakes: 11.65 in - vented cast-iron disc - single-piston caliper.
  • Tires: BF Goodrich Comp.
  • Wheels: Cast Aluminum 17 x 9.5 - 5-spoke.
  • Exterior: No fog lights - No power mirrors - Custom Hood - White.
  • Interior: No AC - No radio - No power windows - No power locks - No sound insulation.

More Information


Since the success of the 1993 Cobra R, Ford decided to create another Cobra R in 1995, this time for the SN95 platform, this time it added more cosmetical differences, and made it look more agressive. This time they upgraded the engine, which instead of being the old 5.0L V8, they swapped it with a new and powerful 5.8L V8. They also changed the fuel system, by adding a 22-gallon fuel cell system, so the car could run for longer periods of time in the track. As it was becoming a tradition, they only offered this car in one color option, this time being "Crystal White" the only color available for the 1995 Cobra R. Ford only produced a total of 250 1995 Cobra R. Now, this car was more expensive then the 1993 Fox-Body Cobra R.
