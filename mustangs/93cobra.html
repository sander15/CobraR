1993 Mustang Cobra R
Fox Body

  • Engine: 5.0L V8
  • Horsepower: 235 hp @ 4,800 rpm.
  • Torque: 280 lb/ft @ 3,750 rpm.
  • Weight: 3,125 lb.
  • Transmission: Modified T5 5-speed manual.
  • Front Brakes: 13 in - vented cast-iron disc - twin-piston caliper.
  • Rear Brakes: 10.5 in - vented disc - single-piston caliper.
  • Tires: Goodyear
  • Wheels: Cast Aluminum 17 x 8 - 6 spoke.
  • Exterior: No fog lights - Red
  • Interior: No AC - No radio - No power windows - No power locks - No sound insulation.

More Information


After many years of Ford not developing a racing or performance Mustang, they decided to create a track Mustang in 1993, and called it the Cobra R, these removed all the extra stuff that was not needed, and just focus on power. The first Cobra R, was developed in the Fox-Body platform, this Mustang had better suspension, cooling, engine, brakes, and many other performance enhancements. These Cobra R came only available in Vibrant Red. Ford only made 107 of these 1993 Cobra R.
